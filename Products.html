Shade Taking




Why are you coming to see us?

Your Dentist has asked you to come and see us so that we can ensure your restoration will be the correct shade. At this stage you can also discuss any thoughts you have as to how you would like your final restoration to look.


What to expect

You will be seen by a Technician who will take your shade and photograph your teeth for reference when your restoration is being fabricated.

Dentists are committed to ensuring the success of your new restoration and your future oral health. Your new restoration is a custom made appliance specifically for you. It is made by a fully qualified Dental Technician who is registered with the General Dental Council.
